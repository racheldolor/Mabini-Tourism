About Mabini, Batangas

Mabini, Batangas is a world-renowned destination for diving and snorkeling, especially in Anilao. Named after Apolinario Mabini, this coastal town offers crystal-clear waters, vibrant reefs, and a warm community that has welcomed visitors for decades. From beginner snorkelers to seasoned divers, Mabini's rich marine ecosystem promises unforgettable underwater adventures.

Getting to Mabini

By Car: 2–3 hours from Manila via SLEX and Lipa-Batangas Road. Well-maintained highways make for a comfortable journey.

By Bus: Take a bus to Batangas Grand Terminal, then jeepney to Mabini. Regular trips available throughout the day.

Best Time to Visit: November to May for calm seas and perfect diving conditions. Year-round destination with varying seasonal experiences.
