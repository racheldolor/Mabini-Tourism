Accommodation in Mabini

Mabini offers a diverse range of accommodation options to suit every budget and preference. From beachfront luxury resorts to cozy budget-friendly hotels, eco-lodges to family-run guesthouses, you'll find the perfect place to relax after your underwater adventures. Most resorts feature diving packages and direct beach access to popular dive sites.

Tips for Choosing Your Resort

  • Consider proximity to dive shops if you plan to dive frequently
  • Check if the resort offers dive packages or equipment rental
  • Look for resorts with good reviews on beaches and water quality
  • Verify amenities like WiFi, AC, and hot water availability
  • Book during shoulder season (November or April-May) for better rates
  • Peak season (December-March) requires advance booking
  • Ask about included meals and breakfast options
  • Check cancellation policies before booking
  • Read recent guest reviews on booking platforms
