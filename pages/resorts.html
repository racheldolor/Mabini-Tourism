Booking Tips

Plan around seasons, amenities, and dive access.

1️⃣

Pick Your Season

Peak (Dec–Mar) needs early booking; Shoulder (Apr–May, Nov) balances weather and price; Off-season (Jun–Oct) has discounts but rougher seas.

2️⃣

Check Dive Access

Choose resorts with on-site dive shops or quick boat rides. Ask about gear rental, rinse areas, and night dive options.

3️⃣

Confirm Essentials

Verify WiFi, AC, hot water, breakfast, and transport. Family stays benefit from pools and larger rooms.

4️⃣

Read Recent Reviews

Check updated guest feedback on cleanliness, service, and water access. Prioritize recent comments.

5️⃣

Reserve Early

Popular weekends and holidays fill fast. Reserve with clear cancellation terms.

Common Amenities

  • On-site or partner dive shop with rental gear
  • Boat access to nearby reefs and house reef entries
  • WiFi, AC, hot showers, and breakfast options
  • Restaurants, bars, and sunset lounges
  • Pools or beach access for downtime
  • Spa or massage services for post-dive recovery
  • Airport/van transfers and tour desks
  • Camera rinse stations for underwater photographers
