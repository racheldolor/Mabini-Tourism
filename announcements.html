Tips for Attending Events in Mabini

  • Book accommodations early during festival season (December-January)
  • Expect crowded roads and public transportation during major events
  • Bring cash - not all vendors accept cards during street festivals
  • Wear comfortable shoes and sun protection for outdoor events
  • Respect local customs and traditions during religious celebrations
  • Try local food and snacks offered at festival stalls
  • Bring a camera to capture memorable moments
  • Check weather forecasts and plan accordingly
  • Follow instructions from event organizers and local authorities
  • Stay hydrated and take breaks during long outdoor events
